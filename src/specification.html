Frequently Asked Questions – Specifications

Explore detailed insights about the premium materials, smart features, and luxury fittings that define the essence of The Pinnacle.

What makes the specifications at The Pinnacle truly luxurious?

The Pinnacle residences feature high-end specifications such as imported marble flooring, premium wooden doors, modular kitchens with built-in appliances, and fully automated smart home systems — combining comfort with class.

Are the apartments fully furnished or semi-furnished?

The luxury apartments at The Pinnacle are semi-furnished, including modular kitchens, branded fittings, designer false ceilings, and air-conditioning in all bedrooms and living areas.

What kind of flooring and finishes are offered in the project?

All living and dining areas come with imported marble flooring, while bedrooms feature premium laminated wooden flooring. Bathrooms are fitted with anti-skid tiles and glass enclosures with top-brand sanitary fittings.

Is there smart home automation in every apartment?

Yes, all units at The Pinnacle are enabled with smart home features, including mood lighting, smart door locks, video door phones, and remote climate control systems.

Which brands are used for electrical and sanitary fittings?

The project integrates reputed brands like Schneider and Legrand for electricals, and Kohler, Jaquar, and Grohe for bathroom fittings, ensuring long-lasting quality and performance.
