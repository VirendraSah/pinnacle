Frequently Asked Questions – Amenities at The Pinnacle

Everything you need to know about your lifestyle experience.

What makes the clubhouse at The Pinnacle stand out?

The Pinnacle features a 1 lakh sq.ft. grand clubhouse designed with 5-star hospitality standards, offering a gym, spa, bar, library, infinity pool, and much more.

Are there any sports facilities within the campus?

Yes, The Pinnacle includes professional-grade courts for cricket, tennis, squash, and badminton—ensuring a balanced, active lifestyle.

What wellness features are available for residents?

Residents enjoy dedicated wellness zones including a spa, meditation deck, yoga areas, medical room, and 24/7 ambulance support.

How secure is the property for families?

The Pinnacle is equipped with smart security: 24x7 CCTV, biometric lifts, intercoms, smart lock systems, and manned entry points.

Is there a sky garden or rooftop experience?

Yes, all towers feature a rooftop Sky Garden, offering panoramic views and open-air walkways for peaceful living above the skyline.
