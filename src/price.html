What is the possession status and expected handover date?

The Pinnacle Mohali is under construction, with possession expected by 2028. Construction updates are regularly shared for full transparency.

Who is the official partner for buying apartments in this project?

Where is The Pinnacle Mohali located?

The project is located in Alpha IT City, Sector 83, Mohali, near top tech campuses, Chandigarh Airport, Amity & Plaksha Universities, and Infosys campus.

What are the key highlights of this project?

Iconic 36-storey towers, 1 lakh sq. ft. clubhouse, skywalk experience, 90% green area, and ultra-modern luxury amenities ideal for luxury living in Mohali.

Are there any current launch offers or benefits?

Yes, exclusive pre-launch offers are available for early buyers of 4 BHK units. Prices will be revised post-launch. Contact Realty Nivesh to get current pricing and offers.

Can I visit the site or book an appointment?

Yes, Realty Nivesh arranges site visits and walk-throughs. Call us at +91-96973-00066 to schedule your visit or request a brochure.

What is the role of STJ Group and Mohali City Centre in this project?

Is The Pinnacle Mohali part of the MCC Mohali City Centre branding?

Yes. The Pinnacle is the crown jewel in the MCC (Mohali City Centre) portfolio—bringing STJ Group’s legacy in commercial development into luxury residential living.

Who is the developer behind The Pinnacle Mohali?
