Floor Plan FAQs – The Pinnacle, Mohali

Answers to common queries about configurations, availability, furnishings, and future launches.

What types of apartments are available at The Pinnacle Mohali?

We offer luxury 3 BHK, 4 BHK, 5 BHK apartments, and penthouse Sky Villas. Each configuration is designed to meet varying space and lifestyle needs.

Are all apartment configurations currently available for booking?

Currently, select 4 BHK apartments in Crest and Zenith towers, and 5 BHK residences in Everest Tower are available for booking. Other configurations will be launched in phases.

What is the typical size range of the apartments?

3 BHK: ~2,400-2,650 sq. ft.
4 BHK: 2,600–2,800 sq. ft.
5 BHK: ~3,400–4,000+ sq. ft.
Sky Villas: To Be Announced

Can I download floor plans for each unit type?

Yes, downloadable 2D floor plans and 3D visual layouts are available for each unit. Click “Download Floor Plan” next to the preferred configuration.

Are penthouses or duplex units available?

Yes, Sky Villas (duplex penthouses) will be launched soon and are planned across all towers. Register your interest to be notified.

What is the expected handover date for booked units?

The project is under construction with expected handover by 2028. Towers will be delivered in phases.

Can I customize the interiors or modify the layout?

Basic layout customizations may be possible in select phases before finishing begins. Speak with our advisory team for more details.
